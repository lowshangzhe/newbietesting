Working as Sales Agent

As a person who would like to be honest, I do not come out of the house a lot. But, I get better memories of the time that I spent working with people. During the time that I am searching for jobs on the internet, I decided to try something else. I have decided to take a different type of a job, and that is selling.


My job is to sell home made cakes to people in the restaurant and on the street. In my first few days, I was given proper training from my new company. I feel grateful that I get to meet a person who has taught me a lot in marketing and to the point of that giving up his own time in getting the sale to mentor me in getting more buyers.


I have learned tons of stuff like how to get more people to buy from crowded areas, getting the right time to refurbish your stock, and knowledge about cakes, and many more.


My mentor has even taught me his expertise in other areas like cars and cooking. He is the most enthusiastic marketer that I have known from the company.

Working in Office
2015 - 2019

Employed in the year 2015, but I can still remember the tension that was in me during the first few weeks, especially during the day of my first job interview. I made tons of mistakes during my first job as a person who never used to work for an office job. Here are my experience in working in an office.


My experience in working with the company shows me that to perform well in any new environment is the importance of making some mistakes as soon as you could. While learning to do, it is wise not to burden yourself with the anxiety of thinking of the ways that you would mess up without proper stop. I have to make more of the same mistake than anyone in the office to learn the fact. I would advise anyone to pay attention to only the three to two areas while working for a particular task.


Another lesson that I have made is that you would be working with other people for the rest of your working hours. Therefore, it is advisable to invest time in knowing other people and their work. I have experienced a lot of lack of communication with my colleague, and it was a disaster. You may have a family background that restricts you from doing certain things with friends. But, you should be the only last person to judge the situation and not your parents.


Lastly, you are encouraged to argue with other people. After wasted part of my life of constantly taking orders from other people, I finally realize that arguments are fundamentals in any personal relationship or a healthy working environment. Therefore, I wish people who have been like me would get the courage to speak up.
